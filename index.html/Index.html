Lesion

Lesion utilizes a tripwire trap that sets off poisonous needles that slow, and deteriate an enemys health. The enemy can remove the needles but it takes 2 seconds. This gives the oppertunity for Lesion to strike quickly, if hes paying attention to his traps. And allows for a suppressive to a rush attack. Lesion usually deploys traps within in 15 feet of target area.

Pulse

Pulse ultilizes a Heart-Beat Sensor that picks up enemies heart beats and displays there eactly location with in 10 feet on a screen. Pulse is good to detect eneimes before they enter the building/target area. If used properly, Pulse can detect enemies and get substantiate amount of wall kills.

Mira

Mira deploys a reflection Black Mirror. Its bullet proof and is only visible from the deployed side. You can knock down the mirror by shooting the C02 canister attatched. Mira is good for a scare tactic, and if your quick enough, a good drop on approaching enemy teams.

Rook

Rooks special is simple. He deploys a body armor pack that gives your team an extra 100 health. He moves slow, and is usually an assist operator.

Jaeger

Jaeger Deploys and Active-Defence System that shoots flying objects such as grenades, flashbangs, and or rockets that enter a 15 foot visible room. Jaeger is quick, and comes with very powerful firearms meant for longer range.

Defence

With Lesion being able to place 3 Trip-Mine poison traps, Pulse is able to detect with his Heart Beat Sensor easier due to increased Heart Rate from the trap itself. The best defence is being able to see your enemy before they see you. Mira is a good way to have full view of your surroundings outside of the room, by deploying the Bullet Proof Black Mirror. Also being able to take bombardments is key to success. Rooks armor plates and Jaegers Active Defence System will allow more key protection during a rush Attack. This set up is more of an aggressive set up, but a good one to make an easy win.

Blitz

Blitz Utilizes a Shield with a Flash Burst in front that temporarly blinds the target 3 feet infront of him. He is useful for a rush tactic but usually plays a good defence for other aggressive operators to hide behind. Blitz rolls in first.

Sledge

Sledge utilizes a sledge hammer that can instantly tear down any breakable wall. With high firepower, and high armor, sledge is a one man wrecking crew who, if used right, can put a serious dent into the enemies team defences, and operators. `

Glaz

Glaz utilizes a Heat Detection Scope that is perfect for laying down heavy amount of smokes and being able to clearly see your target through it. Glaz is a grear suppresive player as one shot is a kill with his highpowered sniper rifle.

Ash

Ash uses a long range breaching round that fires slowley but can destroy an entire wall. Ash is a great rush player as you can burst through walls aggresivley and suprise enemy players quickly. Her weapons shoot at high RPM and can do quick damage.

Thatcher

Thatchers special is an EMP grenade that knocks out all power to lights, and any enemy team defences. Thatcher also carries a high power assault rifle that does loads amount of damage quickly. If used properly, Thatcher can make it easy for your team to decend upon the enemy team and make for a quick Rush defence.

Utilizing the EMP grenades, and making sure glaz has full view of windows from the out side, Ash, Sledge and Blitz can make a safe entry from all corners, pinning enemy teams down.
